Step by step

How we get you live

Four steps from intro call to all agents live. Most clients are done in under a week.

01
Day 1 — 30 minutes

Discovery Call

  • We learn your business — services, team size, current tools, biggest pain points
  • We walk through the right tier for your situation
  • You leave with a clear picture of what gets built and what it costs
02
Days 1–3

Setup & Configuration

  • We connect your Gmail, Google Sheets, Calendly, and Twilio
  • Configure all agents with your business details — your tone, your services, your pricing
  • Set up your dashboard with your team's login credentials
  • Test every workflow end to end with real data
03
Days 3–5

Go Live

  • Your lead form goes live with the webhook connected
  • First real lead comes in — agent responds within 3 minutes
  • You watch the AI agent feed in the dashboard as it works
  • We stay on to fix anything that needs adjusting
04
Month 1 and beyond

Ongoing

  • Agents run 24/7 — leads, follow-ups, proposals, invoices
  • Monthly performance report delivered automatically
  • Strategy call (Tier 3) to find new things to automate
  • Add custom agents as your needs grow
