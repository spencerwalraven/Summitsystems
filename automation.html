What is a custom agent?

It reasons through the situation. Not just the happy path.

An agent is an AI that watches for a trigger, reads your data, makes a decision, and takes action — sending an email, updating a record, generating a document, or firing another agent entirely.

It's not a template or a flowchart. It understands context. It handles exceptions. It knows when something needs a human and when it doesn't.

The difference between a basic automation and an agent: your agent reads the situation the same way a smart employee would.

👁
Trigger Something happens A new review, a scheduled date, a weather forecast, a missed appointment — anything can be a trigger.
🧠
Agent Thinks It reads the context The agent reads your data, evaluates conditions, and decides the right action — not just the scripted one.
⚡
Action It does the work Sends a message, generates a document, updates your CRM, alerts your team, or triggers the next step.
✅
Result Logged and done Every run is recorded. You see what fired, when, and what it did. No black boxes.
Example Agents

Built for the way service businesses actually work

These are real agents we've built or can build in days. Your workflow could be next.

🌦 Retention

Seasonal Maintenance Reminder

Triggers: Each spring and fall, automatically

Sends personalized maintenance reminders to past clients based on the work done for them. "Your deck stain is about 2 years old — here's a quote for a refresh." Turns past jobs into future revenue without lifting a finger.

Estimated value: $5,000–15,000 in repeat revenue per year
⛈ Scheduling

Weather Delay Agent

Triggers: When forecast shows rain or extreme temps on a job day

Monitors the forecast for every scheduled job. When bad weather is coming, it automatically texts the client and crew with a reschedule — no more morning scrambles or awkward calls.

Saves 2–4 hours of scrambling per weather event
📦 Procurement

Materials Price Alert

Triggers: Daily price check against your target thresholds

Checks supplier pricing daily for lumber, copper, HVAC components, or whatever you track. Alerts you the moment prices hit your target so you can lock in materials before estimates get blown. Stop guessing.

Protects margins on every large materials order
🔧 Client Relations

Warranty & Callback Tracker

Triggers: Approaching warranty expiration date on any completed job

Tracks warranty windows on every completed job. Sends a proactive check-in before the warranty expires — asking if everything is holding up. Catches issues before they become complaints or disputes.

Prevents costly callbacks and reputation damage
📋 Procurement

Supplier Quote Collector

Triggers: When you need materials bids for a job

Emails your preferred suppliers, collects their responses, and summarizes the best options for you to review — all in one place. No more chasing quotes across your inbox. Just pick the best option.

Cuts quote-gathering time from hours to minutes
👷 Team Ops

Crew Daily Briefing

Triggers: Every morning at 6 AM

Sends each crew member their job for the day: address, client name, scope, special notes, and the weather forecast for the site. Everyone shows up prepared. No group chats. No confusion.

Eliminates start-of-day confusion and late arrivals
📞 Lead Recovery

No-Show Recovery

Triggers: Immediately when a consultation no-show is detected

If a client misses a consultation, the agent fires instantly: reschedule email goes out, follow-up text fires 2 hours later, and the owner gets an alert if still no response after 24 hours. You don't lose a single lead to forgetfulness.

Recovers 40–60% of no-show appointments
⭐ Reputation

Review Response Agent

Triggers: When a new review appears on your Google Business profile

Drafts a personalized, on-brand response for your approval the moment a review comes in — or posts automatically, your choice. Never let a review sit unanswered again. Shows every prospect you're paying attention.

Boosts review engagement and closes more leads
🏛 Project Mgmt

Permit Status Checker

Triggers: Weekly check on all active job permits

Checks the permit portal status for every active job on your schedule. Alerts you the same day approval comes through so you can book the crew immediately. Stop losing days waiting to find out something already happened.

Eliminates days of delay per job, per permit
📊 Reporting

Custom Monthly Report

Triggers: First of every month, automatically

Builds whatever report matters to your business: crew productivity, material cost by job type, revenue by service category, average job margin — any metric, any format. Lands in your inbox without you asking for it.

Turns data into decisions, every month, automatically
🎂 Referrals

Client Birthday & Anniversary

Triggers: Client birthday or project completion anniversary date

Sends a personal note on client birthdays or on the anniversary of their project. It's a small touch that almost no contractor does — and it drives more referrals than almost anything else. People remember who remembered them.

Measurably increases referral rate over 12 months

Subcontractor Rating Tracker

Triggers: After every completed job involving a subcontractor

Logs how each sub performed on timeline, quality, and communication — automatically, after every job. Builds a running rating over time so you always know who to call first, who to avoid, and who deserves more work.

Builds the data to staff your best jobs with your best people
