What is a custom agent?

AI that reads, decides, and acts — automatically.

An agent is an AI that reads your data, makes decisions, and takes action — automatically. It can send emails, update your CRM, check the weather, pull pricing, generate documents, or trigger any workflow.

If your team does it repeatedly, we can automate it.

👁️
Reads your dataMonitors your CRM, calendar, email, or any connected source
🧠
Makes a decisionUses AI logic to evaluate conditions and determine the right action
⚡
Takes actionSends emails, updates records, generates documents, triggers workflows
📋
Logs everythingEvery action is recorded so you can see exactly what ran and when
