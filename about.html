About spec AI

Three people who got tired of watching good businesses lose money to bad systems

Spencer, Chase, and Ezra came together with one purpose — to help small businesses run more efficiently and give them a fighting chance against the big players. They saw talented tradespeople and service businesses losing work not because of their craft, but because of the chaos of running the business. Missed follow-ups, unsent proposals, invoices that never went out. spec AI was built to handle all of that automatically — meeting business owners where they are, caring for them, and supporting them with the administrative systems they need to thrive.

Why we built this

We kept seeing the same problem everywhere

Great work. Broken systems.

A roofer with 5-star craftsmanship losing leads because he didn't respond fast enough. A remodeler sending proposals a week after the site visit. A landscaper who finished beautiful work but never asked for a single review. The quality of work was excellent. The business operations were holding them back.

Tools that don't fit.

The tools that were supposed to help were either too complicated, too expensive, or too generic. A $300/month CRM that nobody on the team actually uses. A project management app that takes longer to update than just doing the work. Software built for tech companies, not for people who work with their hands.

So we built something different.

Not a software platform you have to learn. Not a consultant who gives advice and leaves. A complete operations system that we install, configure, and run inside your business. Built on tools you already use. Powered by AI that sounds like your team, not a robot. Ready in a week, not a quarter.

Now we're here.

We spent months building, breaking, and rebuilding 31 automation scenarios until they worked right. We tested them with real data, real edge cases, and real expectations. Now we're bringing that system to service businesses everywhere, and building custom automation for anyone with a repetitive task that shouldn't exist.
