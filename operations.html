15 AI agents organized across six functional areas — from the moment a lead submits a form to the moment you collect the final payment.

📥 Lead Management
Turn every lead into a booked appointment — automatically
No lead sits unanswered. The moment a form is submitted, the Lead Agent fires — scoring, responding, and scheduling follow-ups without any human input.
Lead Agent
Scores every new lead as Hot, Warm, or Cold based on project details, budget, and timeline. Fires within 3 minutes of form submission.
Auto-scoring
Welcome Agent
Sends a personalized, AI-written first response to every lead — referencing their specific project and setting expectations for next steps.
Personalized outreach
Email Reply Handler
Reads incoming replies, understands context, and responds intelligently. Flags anything that needs human attention and logs the full thread.
Two-way email
SMS Agent
Two-way SMS via Twilio. Handles lead texts, follow-up confirmations, and appointment reminders — all automated.
Twilio SMS
Nurture Agent
Runs a 5-touch email sequence for leads that don't book immediately. Spaced 2, 5, 10, 21, and 45 days out. Stops the moment they reply.
5-touch sequence
Smart Orchestrator
Coordinates between agents to prevent duplicate outreach, route leads to the right next step, and escalate anything that's gone quiet too long.
Routing & escalation
📋 Sales Pipeline
Proposals and contracts that close while you're on the job
Enter job details once after your site visit. The system generates a branded proposal, sends it to the client, follows up automatically, and handles the contract when they say yes.
Pricing & Estimate Agent
Calculates an estimate range from your pricing tiers based on project type, square footage, materials, and labor. No spreadsheet hunting required.
AI estimates
Proposal Agent
Generates a fully branded proposal in Google Docs — scope of work, pricing, timeline, and payment terms — and emails it to the client the same day as the visit.
Google Docs output
Contract Agent
Creates a complete contract on proposal acceptance — with scope, pricing, and legal terms filled in. Sends for e-signature and follows up automatically if unsigned.
E-signature ready
Change Order Agent
Handles scope changes mid-project. Generates a change order document, gets client approval, and updates the job record and invoice automatically.
Mid-project changes

Every job planned before the crew shows up
Contract signing triggers the Project Planning Agent. Within minutes, your team has a full project plan — phases, materials, assignments, and a kickoff document — before day one.
Project Planning Agent
Builds a full project plan on contract signing — phases, milestones, estimated hours per phase, materials list, and crew assignment recommendations.
AI project plans
Sub Confirmation Agent
Notifies subcontractors of their assignments via email or SMS. Collects confirmations, tracks responses, and flags anyone who hasn't confirmed 48 hours out.
Sub coordination
Learning Agent
Tracks completion times, cost vs. estimate, and client satisfaction scores across all jobs. Feeds insights back to improve future estimates and planning.
Continuous improvement
